Follow the White Rabbit into the Ring | 2019

Digital 3D, Meshmixer

Digital 3D sculpture I created in Meshmixer by sculpting different assets together. The work is a lighthearted, surrealist piece inspired by Alice in Wonderland. The white rabbit, as are the playing cards are key themes in alluding to the famous tale.
